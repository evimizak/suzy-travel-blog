About

Hi, I'm Suzy.

◆  Writer · Photographer · Permanent Wanderer  ◆

I grew up wedged between the seven hills of Lisbon, where the trams clang outside my grandmother's kitchen and the smell of grilled sardines drifts up from the river every June. I was always going to be a writer; I just didn't know I'd write most of it from buses, ferries, and the back rooms of small restaurants where someone's aunt is doing the cooking.

Wander With Suzy started in 2024 as a notebook for friends — a place to keep the recipes, the train timetables, and the half-thoughts I scribbled on napkins. Two years later it's somehow turned into this: stories from forty-two countries, a list of restaurants I'd happily fly back for tomorrow, and the slowly-growing conviction that the best part of any trip is the conversation you didn't plan to have.

— Suzy
